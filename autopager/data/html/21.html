We manufacture a carrier bag range, primarily to the Nordic grocery market.

Wavy top varigauge

Carrier bags which upper part is shaped like a sine curve with a reinforced handle provides a sturdy and strong carrier bag.

Vest style carriers

Carrier bags with vest style handle. Strong bags manufacture in higher volumes. Our modern machinery enables us to produce carrier bags in a cost-effective manner for our customers.
